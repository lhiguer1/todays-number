Who

David Lynch is an American filmmaker, visual artist, and actor.

What

API endpoints to get the number of any given day according to the David Lynch series, David Lynch Theater Presents: TODAY'S NUMBER IS... where each day he picks a random number from 1 to 10 using a jar containing ten numbered ping-pong balls.

How

Each video in the series is ran through the Google Speech Recognition API. The transcript of the video is processed to extract the date and number. All the info is added to a database.

Why

It's cool.
